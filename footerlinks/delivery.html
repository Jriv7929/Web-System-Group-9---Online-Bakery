Delivery Information

Everything you need to know about our delivery service

Delivery Options

Standard Delivery

Delivery Time: 1-2 business days
Fee: ₱60
Coverage: Metro Manila and nearby provinces
Orders placed before 3 PM will be processed the same day.

Express Delivery

Delivery Time: Same day
Fee: ₱120
Coverage: Metro Manila only
Orders must be placed before 12 PM for same-day delivery.

Store Pickup

Pickup Time: 2 hours after order
Fee: FREE
Hours: Monday-Saturday, 7:00 AM - 7:00 PM
You'll receive a notification when your order is ready.

Delivery Areas

Metro Manila

We deliver to all cities and municipalities in Metro Manila, including Quezon City, Manila, Makati, Taguig, Pasig, Mandaluyong, San Juan, Pasay, Parañaque, Las Piñas, Muntinlupa, Caloocan, Malabon, Navotas, Valenzuela, Marikina, and Pateros.

Nearby Provinces

Standard delivery is available to selected areas in Rizal, Cavite, Laguna, and Bulacan. Please check during checkout if your area is covered.

Delivery Policies

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order status through your account dashboard.

Delivery Instructions

Please provide complete and accurate delivery information including landmarks and contact numbers. Our riders will contact you upon arrival.

Failed Delivery

If delivery fails due to incorrect address or recipient unavailability, we will attempt to contact you. A second delivery attempt may incur additional fees.

Quality Assurance

All items are carefully packaged to maintain freshness during delivery. If you receive damaged or unsatisfactory products, please contact us immediately at (555) 123-4567.

Special Conditions

Holidays & Weather

Delivery times may be affected during holidays and adverse weather conditions. We will notify you of any delays and work to deliver your order as soon as possible.

Bulk Orders

For bulk orders or catering services, please contact us at least 3 days in advance. Special delivery arrangements can be made for large orders.
