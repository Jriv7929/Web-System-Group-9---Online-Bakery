Returns & Refunds Policy

Your satisfaction is our priority

Return Policy

Quality Guarantee

At Fresh Pastries Bakery, we are committed to providing the highest quality baked goods. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we want to make it right.

Return Timeframe

Due to the perishable nature of our products, returns or refund requests must be made within 24 hours of delivery or immediately upon pickup.

Eligible Returns

We accept returns or issue refunds for:

  • Products that arrived damaged or stale
  • Incorrect items delivered
  • Missing items from your order
  • Quality issues affecting taste or freshness

Non-Eligible Returns

We cannot accept returns for:

  • Products stored improperly after delivery
  • Change of mind after 24 hours
  • Products consumed or significantly used
  • Custom or personalized orders (unless defective)

How to Request a Return

Step 1: Contact Us

Call us at (555) 123-4567 or email hello@freshpastries.com within 24 hours of receiving your order. Please have your order number ready.

Step 2: Provide Photos

For quality issues, please provide clear photos of the product and packaging. This helps us improve our services and process your return faster.

Step 3: Return Approval

Our customer service team will review your request and approve eligible returns. You'll receive instructions on the next steps via email or phone.

Refund Process

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for purchase:

  • Cash on Delivery: Store credit or bank transfer
  • GCash: Refund to your GCash account
  • Credit/Debit Card: Refund to your card

Refund Timeline

Once your return is approved:

  • GCash refunds: 1-2 business days
  • Credit/Debit card refunds: 5-7 business days
  • Bank transfers: 3-5 business days

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer a partial refund or store credit if only part of your order has quality issues. This will be discussed during the return process.

Exchanges

Product Exchanges

If you received the wrong item or prefer a different product, we can arrange an exchange subject to product availability. Contact us immediately to arrange this.
