Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bakery

Orders & Payments

How do I place an order?

Browse our products, click "Add to Cart" on items you want, then proceed to checkout. Fill in your delivery details and choose your payment method to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), GCash, and Credit/Debit Cards for your convenience.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your order within 2 hours of placing it. Please contact us immediately at (555) 123-4567 or hello@freshpastries.com.

Delivery & Pickup

What are your delivery areas?

We deliver to Metro Manila and nearby provinces. Express same-day delivery is available for Metro Manila orders placed before 12 PM.

How much is the delivery fee?

Standard delivery (1-2 days) costs ₱60. Express same-day delivery is ₱120. Store pickup is free!

What are your store hours for pickup?

Our store is open Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays.

Products & Quality

Are your products freshly baked daily?

Yes! All our breads, pastries, and cakes are baked fresh every day using the finest ingredients.

Do you have allergen information?

Yes, each product page includes dietary information. Most items contain gluten and dairy. Please contact us for specific allergen concerns.

How long do your products stay fresh?

Bread: 2-3 days, Pastries: 1-2 days, Cakes: 3-5 days when refrigerated. We recommend consuming items as fresh as possible for best quality.

Custom Orders

Do you accept custom cake orders?

Absolutely! We can create custom cakes for birthdays, weddings, and special events. Please contact us at least 3 days in advance.

Can I order in bulk?

Yes, we offer bulk orders for events and catering. Please email us at hello@freshpastries.com or call (555) 123-4567 for bulk pricing.

Account & Support

Do I need an account to order?

No, you can checkout as a guest. However, creating an account lets you track orders and save your favorite items.

How do I contact customer service?

You can reach us at (555) 123-4567, email hello@freshpastries.com, or visit our Contact page.
